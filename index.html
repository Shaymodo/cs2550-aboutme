My Personal Interests

I enjoy martial arts a lot. I was an instructor for a kenpo karate studio for a few years and loved it. It helped my learn self defense, but also more "performance" arts with weapons such as nunchucks and swords

I love doing puzzles. I have solved a 3x3 rubiks cube in 15 seconds in the past, and can still generally solve one in about 45 seconds. I also enjoy solving the many variants of rubiks cubes. Another type of puzzle I enjoy are Hanayama cast puzzles,which mostly involves taking pieces of metal apart then putting them back together in the proper way.

I like to do spray paint art as well. I love doing space art the most, and making various sizes and styles of planets. However, as much as I love doing only space art, my favorite piece of art has been painting the moon above a large pine forest with tiny stars in the night sky.
